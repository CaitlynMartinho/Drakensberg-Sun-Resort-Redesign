About us

Our Guiding Principles

  • We are dedicated to providing warm, attentive service, ensuring every guest enjoys a comfortable and memorable stay in the heart of the Drakensberg.
  • We strive to craft unique and enriching moments, connecting you with the beauty of our surroundings and the array of experiences our resort offers.
  • Your relaxation is our priority. We offer a serene environment where you can unwind and rejuvenate amidst the majestic Drakensberg Mountains.
  • We deeply appreciate the breathtaking beauty of the Drakensberg and aim to foster a connection with this extraordinary natural landscape.
  • We are committed to sustainable practices, striving to preserve the natural heritage of the Drakensberg for generations to come.
  • Our dedicated team is the heart of the Drakensberg Sun Resort, committed to providing professional and heartfelt service.

Sustainability Efforts

At Drakensberg Sun Resort, we are deeply committed to preserving the natural beauty and ecological integrity of the magnificent Drakensberg region that we are so privileged to call home. We recognize the importance of operating responsibly and minimizing our environmental footprint to ensure that this extraordinary landscape can be enjoyed by generations to come.


Our ongoing efforts in sustainable environmental excellence have been recognized through achievements such as our previous Heritage Platinum Certification and our attainment of the Diamond Classification status - the highest accolade in South Africa - for several consecutive years. This reflects our continuous journey towards a more sustainable future.


We invite our guests to join us in our commitment by embracing eco-conscious practices during their stay, and we are always exploring new ways to enhance our sustainability initiatives. By choosing the Drakensberg Sun Resort, you are supporting a business that values and actively works to protect the remarkable environment of the Drakensberg.

Our History

1969: Southern Sun Hotels Founded

The Southern Sun Hotels group was founded by hotel magnate Sol Kerzner and South African Breweries Limited. This marks the beginning of the larger hospitality group that would eventually include the Drakensberg Sun Resort.

1986: Drakensberg Sun Resort was built

The Drakensberg Sun Resort was established, bringing a significant leisure destination to the heart of the Central Drakensberg. Its construction provided a base for visitors to explore the majestic mountains and enjoy a range of recreational activities within a comfortable and scenic setting.

2009: Drakensberg Sun Resort achieves Heritage Platinum Certification for sustainability

The Drakensberg Sun Resort demonstrated its commitment to environmental responsibility by achieving Heritage Platinum Certification. This recognition highlighted the resort's early adoption of sustainable practices and its dedication to preserving the natural beauty of the Drakensberg region.

2013: Drakensberg Sun Resort undergoes a significant renovation

The year 2013 saw a significant renovation at the Drakensberg Sun Resort. This upgrade aimed to modernize the resort's facilities, enhance the guest experience, and ensure it continued to offer contemporary comfort and amenities within its stunning natural setting.

2012 - April 2022: Parent company known as Tsogo Sun Hotels

Between 2012 and April 2022, the parent company of the Drakensberg Sun Resort operated under the name Tsogo Sun Hotels. During this period, the resort remained a key part of this broader hospitality group, benefiting from its resources and network while continuing to offer its unique Drakensberg experience.

May 2022: Parent company rebrands back to Southern Sun

In May 2022, the parent company underwent a significant rebranding, returning to its original and well-established name, Southern Sun. This change signified a reconnection with the group's foundational identity and its long history within the South African hospitality industry, with the Drakensberg Sun continuing as a valued property within the Southern Sun portfolio.
