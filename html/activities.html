Activities & Services

Adventure & Relaxation In The Drakensberg

From canoeing on our lake to hosting a team building weekend, and from a couple's spa treatment to horse riding with the kids, the Drakensberg Sun Resort is perfect for all types of family holidays, corporate experiences, and adventures in the stunning Central Drakensberg.


You can stay connected with uncapped WiFi, dine in style at the restaurant, relax in front of the fire in the bar, host memorable events in our venues, or explore the best of the Berg with a range of sports and wild-adventure opportunities on offer.

Explore our activities

Canoes & Row Boats

Canoes & Row Boats

Borrow one of our canoes or row boats and explore our private lake. Equipment is provided for you to enjoy this exciting Drakensberg experience.

Recommended in Summer - mornings and afternoons

Playground

Playground

Drakensberg Sun Resort is definitely family-friendly and features a well-equipped play area designed for kids, and hosts a regular Kid’s Programme to give you more time to relax.

Recommended in Summer - mornings and afternoons

Hiking Trail

Hiking Trail

Our 4 on-site hiking trails include the Blue Grotto, Fern Forest, Ellis Hike and the Lake Walk. Hiking in the Drakensberg is as challenging as you would like it to be, with trails for all fitness levels.

Recommended in Summer - mornings and afternoons

Horse Riding

Horse Riding

For a unique experience and view of the Drakensberg, a horse ride is a must. Feel the wind through your hair with either a beginners or advanced horse ride through the mountains.

Recommended in Summer - mornings and afternoons

Trout Fising

Trout Fising

Try your hand at trout fishing in the beautiful Central Drakensberg. Nothing compares to the relaxation afforded by a day’s relaxing trout fishing. Equipment available for rental.

Recommended in Summer - mornings and afternoons

Free WiFi

Free WiFi

Stay with us at Drakensberg Sun Resort and enjoy free unlimited WiFi access per room, per day. Enjoy effortless access and great connectivity during your stay.

Hotel Services

Hotel services and amenities include our gift shop, hospitality lounge, laundry service, and much more. For a full list please contact the hotel directly.

Business Services

Business Services

As well as our meeting and event spaces, business guests enjoy services including photocopying, library with PC / printer and internet access (self-service workstation), boardroom, conference room and facsimile service.

Nearby Activities

Nearby Activities

With our extensive range of onsite amenities and services, there are a number of nearby facilities that guest can enjoy including a coffee shop, Four Rivers Adventures, restaurants, and the Craft Centre.
