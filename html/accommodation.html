Rooms, Suites & Self Catering Chalets

Warm & Welcoming Drakensberg Accommodation

Accommodation at Drakensberg Sun Resort is comfortable, restful, and relaxing. Whether you're here to explore the surrounding natural wonder of the Central Drakensberg, for a team-building retreat, or to enjoy a family holiday, our rooms and suites are fully equipped to make you feel right at home.


With comfortable beds, décor that complements the natural surroundings, and spectacular views of the Cathkin Park mountains and our large gardens, our hotel rooms are designed for your comfort. Whether you choose Standard, Superior, Executive or Presidential, all 78 hotel rooms are well-appointed and en-suite. Room amenities include free uncapped WiFi, air conditioning, a fridge, in-room tea and coffee facilities, DStv, and more. Rates include dinner, bed & breakfast and don't forget, kids under 18 stay and eat breakfast free.

Timeshare

The resort's 30 self-catering chalets are available for members of the Southern Sun Resorts timeshare programme, one of the leading holiday ownership clubs in the country. The resort's two to six sleeper chalets are positioned with the best lake or mountain views and are fully equipped for a memorable holiday in the 'Berg. Each chalet has a private braai area, a full kitchen, relaxing lounge area, and indoor fireplace, while a selection of timeshare holiday apartments in the hotel combine the best of self-catering with hotel comfort.
