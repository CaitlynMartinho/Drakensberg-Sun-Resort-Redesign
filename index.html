Welcome to the Drakensberg Sun Resort

In the heart of the magnificent Drakensberg mountains, the Drakensberg Sun Resort is one of South Africa's most popular and loved country hotel retreats. Surrounded by the beautiful Cathkin Park range, our Drakensberg resort is a relaxing destination for family holidays, corporate events, or a romantic break.


Accommodation choices include a selection of 78 beautifully furnished en-suite hotel rooms, from our comfortable Standard Rooms to the luxury of our Presidential Suite.


Spend your days hiking, horse riding, fishing, relaxing poolside, or in our eco-friendly spa surrounded by breathtaking landscapes. The Drakensberg Sun Resort's selection of holiday amenities includes a private lake for canoeing, tennis courts, multipurpose court and kids play areas, as well as unlimited free WiFi and much more.
